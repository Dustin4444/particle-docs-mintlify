**Particle Network** builds chain abstraction infrastructure. **Universal Accounts** is the headline product — one account and one unified balance that works across every supported chain. **Particle Auth**, **Particle Connect**, the **AA SDK**, and the **Universal Deposit SDK** are composable building blocks you can use independently or alongside Universal Accounts.

## What Particle Network ships

| Product | What it does | Section |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Universal Accounts** | One smart account + unified balance across all chains. Users never bridge. | [Universal Accounts →](/universal-accounts/cha/overview) |
| **Particle Auth** | Social-login wallet creation (Google, Twitter, Discord, etc.) | [Particle Auth →](/social-logins/auth/introduction) |
| **Particle Connect** | Wallet aggregator — one modal for MetaMask, WalletConnect, embedded wallets, and more | [Particle Connect →](/social-logins/connect/introduction) |
| **AA SDK** | ERC-4337 bundler + paymaster infrastructure for full control over smart accounts | [AA SDK →](/aa/introduction) |
| **Universal Deposit SDK** | Accept deposits from any chain — funds auto-bridge to your destination as USDC | [Universal Deposit →](/universal-accounts/ua-reference/universal-deposit/overview) |
