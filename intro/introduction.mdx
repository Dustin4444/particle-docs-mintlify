**Particle Network** builds chain abstraction infrastructure. **Universal Accounts** is the headline product — one account and one unified balance that works across every supported chain. **Particle Auth**, **Particle Connect**, the **AA SDK**, and the **Universal Deposit SDK** are composable building blocks you can use independently or alongside Universal Accounts.
