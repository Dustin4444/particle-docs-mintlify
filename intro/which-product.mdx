Particle Network has five developer-facing products. Most teams start with **Universal Accounts** — but each product can be used independently. Use the guides below to find your fit in under a minute.

---

## Start with your goal

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Universal Accounts" icon="globe" href="/universal-accounts/cha/overview">
    _My dApp should work on any chain — users never bridge._

    One account and one unified balance across all supported chains. Your dApp deploys on one chain; Universal Accounts handles the rest.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Particle Auth" icon="address-card" href="/social-logins/auth/introduction">
    _Users sign up with Google, email, or social — and get a wallet automatically._

    MPC-secured embedded wallet creation behind familiar Web2 login flows. No seed phrases, no browser extension required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Particle Connect" icon="wallet" href="/social-logins/connect/introduction">
    _I need one modal for social logins and external wallets. I can also use AA features._

    A wallet aggregator (similar to RainbowKit) that unifies external wallets and Particle Auth under one UI component.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AA SDK" icon="bolt" href="/aa/introduction">
    _I need to add AA features to my dApp._

    Sponsor transactions or let users pay gas in ERC-20 tokens.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Universal Deposit SDK" icon="arrow-down-to-bracket" href="/universal-accounts/ua-reference/universal-deposit/overview">
    _I need my app to accept deposits from any chain without asking users to bridge._

    Generates per-user deposit addresses on every supported chain. Funds are automatically detected and bridged to your destination chain in USDC — wallet-agnostic, no Connectkit or Authkit required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Decision table

| | Universal Accounts | Particle Auth | Particle Connect | AA SDK | Universal Deposit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **What users get** | One account + unified balance across chains | Social-login wallet, no seed phrase | One modal for any wallet | Smart account with gas sponsorship + batching | Deposit from any chain, lands as USDC on your chain |
| **What you integrate** | Universal Accounts SDK | Particle Auth SDK | Particle Connect SDK | AA SDK + bundler/paymaster config | Universal Deposit SDK |
| **Best combined with** | Particle Auth or Connect as the signer | Universal Accounts or AA SDK | Universal Accounts or AA SDK | Any signer (Auth, Connect, or external EOA) | Any wallet or auth provider |

<Tip>
  **Can I use Universal Accounts without Particle Auth?**

  Yes. The Universal Accounts SDK is provider-agnostic. You can use Particle Connect, Web3Modal, RainbowKit, or any signer that provides an EOA. Particle Auth is optional.
</Tip>
